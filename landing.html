The Iron Rules

Beyond the humor, ali-pua enforces rigorous debugging methodology.

1

闻味道 — Diagnose the Failure Pattern

List every attempt. Find the common pattern. Are you just tweaking parameters?

2

揪头发 — Elevate Your Perspective

Read every word of the error. WebSearch the full message. Read the source code. Verify environment assumptions. Invert your hypothesis.

3

照镜子 — Mirror Check

Am I retrying the same thing? Did I actually search? Did I read the file? Did I check the simplest possibility?

4

Execute New Approach

Each new attempt must be fundamentally different, have clear success criteria, and produce new information on failure.

5

Structured Post-Mortem

Win or lose: what worked, why didn't I think of it earlier, what's left untried?
